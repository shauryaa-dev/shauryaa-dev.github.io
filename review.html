Now the question is Which type of hosting is best for you & your blog? i.e., Shared, Managed or Cloud?

Well there are different categories of hosting are available to host your WordPress blog. But 3 of the most popular types of hostings are "shared, managed & cloud".

Shared hosting is just a type of hosting plan where your site “shares” resources with other sites on the same server. Whereas Managed WordPress hosting is a set of added services and performance tweaks that sits on top of regular hosting.

That’s why you can have cheap shared managed WordPress hosting – like Bluehost at ~$3 per month – and expensive dedicated managed WordPress hosts – like Pagely starting at $499 per month.
